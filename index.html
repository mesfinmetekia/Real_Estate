About Us

We Are Sheger and we are

Sheger Real Estate is a highly esteemed company with a strong financial foundation, state-of-the-art design innovation, quality and timely delivery. This combination enables us to construct powerful visions and has the potential to evoke an immediate desire to tour the interior. Our company values teamwork and interdisciplinary collaboration as a path to insight and excellence in our work. Our company’s team of committed professionals embrace the following core values:

H.E.A.L.T.H.

Our values are what everyone who works at Sheger Companies strives for each day, and they are applicable to any companies we work with. These values are also the metric we use to decide to invest or get in business with others. We believe our team’s success is determined by our ability to maintain our HEALTH, which is defined by our core values:

• Hard work

• Extraordinary results

• Accountable in all things

• Leaders only

• Teamwork

• Humble
